Facts on the Benefits of Meditation

  • Meditating improves memory and promotes cognative function. A Mayo Clinic study showed that people in their mid 50s who meditate 25 minutes a day had the cognative abilities of a 24 year old.

  • A key benefit to meditation is stress reduction, one of the key reasons people try meditation. With the stressors of the modern world meditation can help us to disconnect from the demands of everyday life.

  • Meditating regulary can help increase your emotional awareness by increasing your self awareness, which can make you the best version of yourself.

  • Another major benefit of regular meditation is it can help you control your anxiety. Numerous studies have shown that meditation greatly increases one's ability to manage anxiety.
